# Weekly Review Workflow

## Purpose

The weekly review turns collected evidence into usable market understanding.

## Inputs

- newly captured evidence
- extracted signals
- suggested links
- existing insight briefs
- content and strategy questions

## Review Steps

### 1. Clear The Inbox

For each new item:

- ignore
- save as evidence
- extract signal
- attach to existing insight
- create new insight

### 2. Merge Duplicate Signals

Look for repeated observations with different wording. Preserve useful original language, but avoid letting duplicates inflate confidence.

### 3. Promote Strong Patterns

Promote a group of signals into an insight when:

- the pattern appears across multiple sources
- the audience is clear
- the implication is useful
- there is enough evidence to explain why it matters

### 4. Link The Graph

Create or approve relationships:

- evidence supports signal
- signal supports insight
- insight relates to audience pain
- insight inspires content idea
- insight affects strategic bet
- signal contradicts insight

### 5. Write Or Refresh Briefs

For each strong insight, update:

- thesis
- evidence
- confidence
- contradictions
- content angles
- offer implications
- open questions

### 6. Choose Actions

End the review with:

- 1 to 3 content ideas to explore
- 1 strategic question to investigate
- 1 source gap to fill
- any weak insight that needs more evidence

## Weekly Output

```text
Top Emerging Insight:

Strongest Evidence Cluster:

Most Interesting Contradiction:

Best Content Opportunity:

Strategy Implication:

Research Gap:

Next Capture Targets:
```
